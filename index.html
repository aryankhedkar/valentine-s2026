A note for you

I wrote you something

I'm not great with words, you know that. But you deserve the effort, so here goes.

Sofia,

A year ago today I married you. I'd do it again tomorrow and the day after that and every day after that. But honestly, I knew I was going to spend my life with you long before the wedding. I think I knew somewhere around the time I realised that no one else makes me laugh the way you do, no one else drives me as mad as you do, and no one else makes a random Tuesday evening feel like the best part of my week.

You're the most chaotic person I've ever met and I mean that as the biggest compliment I can give. You bring energy into every room, you turn nothing plans into the best memories, and you somehow make even the most stressful days feel manageable just by being around. I don't know how you do it. I really don't. But I'm so glad you do.

I love the mornings where we take the tube together to Kings Cross and I watch you disappear towards Holborn while I head to Farringdon, and for those few minutes on the platform I just think about how lucky I am that I get to go home to you later. It's such a small, ordinary thing. But it's my favourite part of the day and I don't think you even know that.

You've made me a better person this year. More patient (still working on it), more present, more aware of what matters. You challenge me when I need it, you support me when I don't ask for it, and you love me in a way that I genuinely don't think I deserve but I'm going to spend the rest of my life trying to.

Thank you for four incredible years. Thank you for saying yes three years ago. Thank you for marrying me last year. And thank you for being the reason I look forward to every single day.

All my love, always. Aryan

Our story

How we got here

Every milestone on the same date. I don't think that's a coincidence.

14 February 2022
Our first Valentine's Day
Two months after we met. This was the night that made it real. The night I knew this wasn't just something casual, that you weren't just someone I liked spending time with. You were the person. I just didn't have the words for it yet.
14 February 2023
I asked you to marry me
One year in and I already knew I didn't want to do any of this without you. So I asked. And you said yes. I don't think I've ever felt relief and joy hit me at the same time like that before. Probably the best moment of my life up to that point.
2023 to 2025
Building towards forever
Two years of planning, growing, learning each other's quirks (you have a lot of them, by the way). Late night conversations about everything and nothing. The kind of time where you don't realise how good it is until you look back on it. I'm looking back on it now, and it was really, really good.
14 February 2025
We got married
Valentine's Day. Our day. The day I stood next to you and made the easiest promise of my entire life. I remember looking at you and thinking, "yeah, this is it. This is the person." And it was. It is. It always will be.
14 February 2026
One year married, four years us
365 days of marriage. Over 1,500 days of you. Every single one of them worth it. We've built something real, something that's ours, and I'm so proud of what we are. This is just the beginning and I can't wait for the rest of it.
